Brand 01 — Social Media Campaign
Social Media

Growing a brand from zero to recognisable.

The Brief: A new F&B brand with no social presence needed to establish authority and build a loyal community within 6 months.

Our Approach: We developed a content universe grounded in brand culture — not just products. Weekly shoots, daily content, paid amplification, and micro-influencer partnerships.

The Result: 28,000 followers in 5 months, 3.8% average engagement rate, 4x increase in monthly footfall.

Brand 02 — Brand Film
Brand Film

A film that redefined a category.

The Brief: A heritage real estate brand wanted a brand film that positioned them as modern without abandoning their 30-year legacy.

Our Approach: A 90-second cinematic film using archival storytelling techniques, shot over 2 days across Bangalore. Full post production in our suite.

The Result: 1.2M views in 30 days, regional press coverage, and a 40% increase in site enquiries in Q1.
