01
Visual Storytelling · Photography

Krispy Kreme — Joy, frame by frame.

To visually capture the joy and indulgence at the heart of Krispy Kreme through a series of engaging, scroll-stopping images that resonate with today's audience.

We built a visual direction around warm lighting to enhance the "fresh & melt-in-your-mouth" feel, minimal yet vibrant compositions to keep focus on the product, and lifestyle elements to evoke everyday indulgence. Each frame was carefully styled to highlight texture, shine, and softness — then refined in post to ensure colour accuracy aligned with brand guidelines.

A cohesive, premium content library with strong visual consistency across digital channels and enhanced product appeal driving measurable engagement.

02
Personal Brand · Fashion & Couture

Pankaj Soni — Zero to 103K.

Pankaj Soni — a Bangalore-based couturier with a sharp design sensibility — came to us with no digital presence. We were brought in to build his personal brand entirely from zero.

We led with curated content around his bespoke designs, drawing in influencers and celebrities who wore his work organically. Runway appearances across Delhi, Mumbai, and Bangalore positioned him as the face of new-age designer custom clothing.

From zero to a verified 103K community in 5 years, with a ~4% engagement rate and 1,862 posts of consistent brand storytelling. Footfall and client inquiries grew an estimated 4×, expanding the label's footprint across three cities.
