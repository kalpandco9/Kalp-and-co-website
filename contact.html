Common Questions

Answers before you ask.

A few things clients often want to know before we start working together.

How quickly can you turn around a project?
+
Turnaround depends on the scope. A single photography session can be delivered in 3–5 business days. Larger campaigns or brand films typically take 3–6 weeks from brief to final delivery. Because everything is in-house, we're significantly faster than agencies that outsource production.
Do you work with brands outside of Bangalore?
+
Yes. While our studio is based in Bangalore, we work with brands across India. Our team is available for on-location shoots and campaigns in other cities. Digital services like social media and advertising are managed remotely for clients nationwide.
Can you handle our brand end-to-end?
+
Absolutely — that's our core offering. We handle strategy, content creation, photography, videography, graphic design, digital advertising, and outdoor advertising all under one roof. No need to manage multiple vendors or agencies. One team, full accountability.
What industries do you work with?
+
We have experience across F&B, hospitality, real estate, fashion, wellness, retail, lifestyle, and technology. Our creative process adapts to the culture and audience of each category without losing the quality benchmark we hold for every project.
Is there a minimum project size or budget?
+
We don't publish fixed minimums, but we typically work best with brands that see creative as an investment rather than a cost. If you're unsure, start a conversation — we'll be honest with you about whether we're the right fit.
